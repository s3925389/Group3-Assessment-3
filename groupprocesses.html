Group Processes


Reflecting on our first assignment as a group, assignment #2, we believe working together to achieve the same common goal, to complete the assignment by the due date/time, was a great success. We all communicated well together, and meetings were a great success, though we did have some challenges considering everyone has different schedules and circumstances. Everyone was there when someone required help, had any questions or needed someone to offer an opinion.One of the main challenges we faced in assignment #2 was in relation to working on the website through GitHub. We soon noticed that when everyone was adding information and committing at the same time on GitHub, information would get lost or not added as when working on local machines, often commits were made before pulling new changes onto the files. Group members expressed opinions how to approach and make it easier for everyone when it comes to adding their information to the website. For this assignment, a plan has been put in place so while everyone will contribute to the report, when sections are completed, one user will add all the information to the index file, ensuring that everything is added onto the website, and there will be no overriding of the work of others.
