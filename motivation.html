Motivation

There is a growing interest in chatbots. Chatbots are matching agent serving as natural language user interface for data and service provider. Chatbots help users to obtain timely and efficient assistant and information. Most of the people in social environment are increasingly spending time on messaging platforms like Facebook messenger. The Chatbot applications suggest that conversational user interfaces still face substantial challenges, generally speaking, as well as for the field of human computer interaction. Chatbots imply not only a change in the interface between users and technology they also imply changing user dynamics and patterns of use.

If a company needs to know more about how clients experience chatbots and to understand the customer needs, that motivates the future use of chat.The word “chatbot” is a derivative of chat robots, the chatbots as machine agents serve as natural language user interfaces to data and services through text or voice. Chatbot allow users to ask questions.developers have a need to know how the people experience with the chatbot which can motivate the future needs of chatbots.

For this project, we decided to try and create a chatbot as it initially seemed relatively straightforward and simple, and their creation process and use have been around for a while.However, since many of the chatbots currently being used seem mostly to have a very business-like tone, we thought a chatbot that provided a more conversational, everyday type of tone would provide a greater appeal to businesses and customers alike, where people seeking assistance feel more like they are talking to a real person and thus create a better customer experience.
