Chatbot Developer:


Job Description

Team Conquerors need a smart individual that have a great passion and experience in creating a chatbot application for our website. The role includes the development of the product, monitoring and analysing conversional data, and give the user a great experience.


Responsibilities

  • Design conversational chatbot using state of the art technology
  • Clean / analyse data coming from bot conversation
  • Define recurring questions that can be handled automatically
  • Improve dialog flow to handle those recurring questions / develop new actions
  • Help with handling of multilingual support
  • Train the bot using Dialog flow

Requirements

  • 2+ years of RASA Stack
  • Experience on Python, AI welcome, RASA NLU & Core, & Dialog flow
  • Sound Knowledge in AWS is required.
  • Experience working with chatbots.
  • Fundamentals of Natural Language processing.
  • Personal or professional projects on deep learning is a bonus
  • Sense of responsibility and proven leadership

Data Analyst:


Job Description
At Conqueror’s, we are developing a chatbot to satisfy the needs of our users every day. We are building a customer service chatbot to answers our customers questions and solve their problems, concerns, and even get a smile by telling a few jokes. Customers need help outside of normal operating hours across all businesses, the chatbot becomes a first point of contact for our customers experience and the team here at Conqueror’s takes this very seriously, therefore we are expanding the team to build an in-depth and advanced chatbot to take that next step into a pleasurable user experience.


In this role you will help to define the metrics of the chatbot and build reports that monitor the chatbots performance. You will also work together with the Bot Owner, Developer, Copywriters, Sales and marketing, as well as the Quality Assurance Professional to improve its overall intelligence. You will ensure that the chatbot is continuously optimized and expanded to maximize not only the success but the but contribute to the overall user’s experience.


Responsibilities:


  • Analyse performance and user feedback in a professional manner
  • Accurately generate reports for our business and strategy decisions for the chatbot project.
  • Designing data structures (in collaboration with the Developer), to improve the effectiveness of chatbot responses and help overall metrics.
  • Coordinate and work with bot owner/developer to optimize the data flow from raw data into data structures
  • Engaging with the Bot Owner, Developer, Copywriters, Sales and marketing, and the Quality Assurance Professional to ensure that all data is up-to-date according to the latest features and/or integrations.

Requirements:

  • Bachelor’s degree or higher in Computer Science, Business Analytics, Statistics, Information Systems
  • 2+ years of relevant work experience in business data analytics
  • Strong attention to detail
  • Experience in customer service using both digital as well as traditional methods
  • Able to handle difficulties and to rectify complex situations
  • Proficient in Excel with large amounts of data (Python and SQL)
  • Strong Communication and Presentation Skills
  • Experience in AI, NLP, etc. (highly desirable)
  • Experience with chatbots or chatbot conflict resolutions is desirable but not essential
  • Project Management experience is desirable but not essential

Quality Assurance Engineer:


Job Description:
The Conquerors are an up-and-coming chatbot development team dedicated to providing quality customized chatbots to our client's specifications. We are looking for an experienced, enthusiastic and dedicated quality assurance engineer to join the team to assist on our current project which is a customer service FAQ chatbot designed to respond to users' requests for information and provide the appropriate response. You will be working closely with the chatbot developer, bot owner, copywriters and sale and marketing team throughout the development process and beyond to ensure that the product is viable and continually meets quality standards as well as customer's requirements.


Responsibilities:

  • Perform continuous automated and manual testing throughout the development process
  • Identify and set standards and procedures to ensure quality of product
  • Maintain and adapt testing strategies to ensure technical and non-technical requirements are met throughout the development process
  • Create testing scripts for automated testing processes
  • Review and analyse scripts and code for efficiency and malfunctions
  • Find errors and bugs and suggest and implement solutions to rectify issues
  • Document and report on results of testing processes
  • Liaise with and report back to the chatbot developer, bot owner, copywriters and sales and marketing team to ensure all features are viable and meet quality standards

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree or higher in Information Technology, Computer Science, Business Analytics, or related field
  • Minimum 2 years of experience in a quality assurance/tester role
  • Knowledge of the software development lifecycle
  • Knowledge in SQL and scripting
  • Experience in Agile testing practices
  • Experience in testing across different operating systems (iOS, Android, Windows, Linux, etc)
  • Experience in AI and conversational AI chatbot development (desirable but not essential)

You will have:

  • Strong attention to detail
  • Good time management skills
  • Exceptional problem-solving skills
  • Strong communication skills
  • An ability to work individually and as part of a team
