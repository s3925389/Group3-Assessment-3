Landscape


Chatbots are used in all different places of the web or applications. AI ran technology is a source that required interaction with humans and helps them with their enquiry. Many competitors use this technology to help users to navigate throw their systems. There are quite a few systems of chatbots that can perform different tasks that is required to support your line of work. Just to name 3 different systems and what they are capable of archiving are:


HubSpot:
HubSpot is a chat building software which allows users to automate and measure the live chat conversations. This system allows their customers to book meetings, ask specific questions and help to navigate throw out the site.


Watson Assistant:
The system is developed by IBM and is one of the most intelligent chat bots in the market. The chatbot AI is pre-loaded with content that is specific to the company’s industry. The AI can understand call different chats and manage different call logs. It has knowledge base answers that supports the industry. This system runs on websites, messaging channels, customer service tools and mobile apps.


Vergic:
The Vergic chatbot is a customer support agent. The system can handle chats that would lead to something or a dead end, Transactional. The bot then transfers and chat problems to a customer service agent if it becomes too complicated to handle. Verfic can share documents and can manage workflows.

These are some examples what different platforms that help bring chatbots alive. The difference between them and the team project, we will be using RASA Stack, which is an open source on contexts, RASA NLU and RASA Core, to help the team develop our chatbot project. RASA allows us to expand the chatbots voice assistance capabilities.
RASA NLU users’ natural language understanding solution which takes the users input and tried to extract those available entities. Its job is to process and interpret messages been used.

RASA Core manages the solution to try and build a probability model which then decides what actions to perform in the best way of the users input. The messages will come across from NUL and the core decides what should happen next.
