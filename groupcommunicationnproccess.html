Group Communcation and Processes


For this project, it appears that we will require better and greater communication and collaboration for the project development and report write up than in the previous group project given that it will be difficult to assign sections to individual members for the writing of the report. As the team members have not changed, the prior experience we have had working together as a team will help to guide our interactions for this project as we have grown to know how each individual operates and have gained a basic understanding of where each person’s strengths and weaknesses lie.


Given the fact that team members are based in different states, as well as the current COVID environment and the members’ personal and working commitments, we will aim to have scheduled recorded online meetings twice a week to check in and record progress with the project and maintain communications through Microsoft Teams. We believe that following the experience with the first team project, this communication level is sufficient for effective management of the process. The collaboration on the report will also be conducted primarily through the integrated Microsoft Word app on Teams to ensure all necessary information is shared between team members and necessary updates and rectifications can be managed in real time.
